
# Clipoz
The keychain that tracks movement

**What are Clipoz?**
Clipoz are small 3D printable keychains, that track movement. When the movement is triggered between certain times, a notification is sent to select Mates, telling them that your on the move!

**How are clipoz useful?**
Clipoz are perfect for school freinds, who walk the same route to school, but pass eachothers houses at different times. They tell your freind when you leave, without telling them where you are - which removes lots of privacy concens.


**Why did I create Clipoz?**
I created clipoz because I wanted to meet up with my freind at the bus stop without missing him, because he is really forgetful and won't text me. 


**Why do they look like that?**
Clipoz are themed in different fruits, printing in packs of three. I want to make them into a blind box sort of system. maybe one day.
